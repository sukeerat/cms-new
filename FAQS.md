## General Questions

### Q1: What is PlaceIntern?
**A:** PlaceIntern is an Internship Management Portal designed to streamline the entire internship lifecycle for students, faculty supervisors, and institutional administrators. It facilitates internship submission, progress tracking, mentor coordination, and grievance management.

### Q2: How do I access the portal?
**A:** Navigate to https://placeintern.com/login and enter your credentials (Registration Number/Username and Password).

### Q3: What should I do if I forget my password?
**A:** Contact your immediate supervisor:
- **Students:** Contact your Faculty Mentor
- **Faculty:** Contact your Principal
- **Principal:** Contact State Directorate support at dtepunjab.internship@gmail.com

### Q4: Do I need to change my password after first login?
**A:** Yes, the system requires all users to change their password on first login for security purposes. The new password must be at least 6 characters long.

### Q5: What browsers are supported?
**A:** The portal works best on modern browsers like Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge, and Safari. Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version.

### Q6: What is the support email?
**A:** For technical or administrative support, contact: dtepunjab.internship@gmail.com

## Student FAQs

### Login & Access

#### Q1: How do I get my login credentials?
**A:** Your Faculty Mentor will provide your login credentials. The username is typically your Registration Number.

#### Q2: Can I update my profile information?
**A:** Yes, navigate to **Student Profile > Profile** from the sidebar menu to update your personal information, email address, and upload your profile photo.

#### Q3: What should I do after my first login?
**A:** After first login:
1. Change your password when prompted
2. Update your profile with a valid email address
3. Upload your profile photo
4. Review your dashboard to understand your internship status

### Internship Submission

#### Q4: How do I submit my internship details?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Portal > Submit Internship Details** and fill in all required information including company details, duration, and internship type.

#### Q5: Can I have multiple active internships?
**A:** No, you can only have 1 active internship at a time in the system.

#### Q6: What is the minimum internship duration?
**A:** The minimum required internship duration is 16 weeks.

#### Q7: What types of internships can I submit?
**A:** You can submit:
- **Self-Identified Internship:** Internships you found on your own
- **College-Arranged Internship:** Internships arranged by the institution

#### Q8: Can I edit my internship details after submission?
**A:** Once submitted, certain fields may be locked. Contact your Faculty Mentor if you need to make changes to approved internships.

### Documentation

#### Q9: What documents do I need to upload?
**A:** You must upload:
- **Joining Letter/Offer Letter:** Required at the start of your internship
- **Monthly Reports:** One report per month throughout your internship

#### Q10: When are monthly reports due?
**A:** Monthly reports are due once every month. The exact date may be specified by your institution or Faculty Mentor.

#### Q11: How many monthly reports do I need to submit?
**A:** The number of monthly reports equals the number of months in your internship. For example, a 4-month internship requires 4 monthly reports.

#### Q12: What format should my documents be in?
**A:** Documents should typically be in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format. Check file size limits when uploading.

### Tracking & Monitoring

#### Q13: How do I track my internship progress?
**A:** Your dashboard displays 4 key milestones:
1. Internship Data (Complete/Incomplete)
2. Joining Letter (Submitted/Pending)
3. Grievances (Open count)
4. Monthly Reports (e.g., "4/8 Reports Submitted")

#### Q14: Who is my Faculty Mentor?
**A:** Your Faculty Mentor details are displayed on your dashboard. This is the faculty member assigned by your Principal to supervise your internship.

#### Q15: How often will my Faculty Mentor visit?
**A:** Faculty Mentors are required to conduct at least 1 visit per month (physical, virtual, or telephonic) throughout your internship duration.

### Grievances & Support

#### Q16: How do I report an issue with my internship?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Portal > Submit Grievance** to report any concerns related to your internship experience.

#### Q17: What should I do if I have a technical issue with the portal?
**A:** Navigate to **Support & Help > Submit Technical Query** to report system-related issues.

#### Q18: How can I track my submitted queries?
**A:** Navigate to **Support & Help > My Queries** to view the status of all your submitted technical queries.

#### Q19: Can I view my application history?
**A:** Yes, navigate to **Internship Portal > My Applications** to track all your submitted internships.

## Faculty/Teacher FAQs

### Access & Setup

#### Q1: How do I receive my login credentials?
**A:** Your login credentials are sent to your official email address by the Principal or System Administrator.

#### Q2: What is my role in the system?
**A:** As a Faculty Supervisor (also called Teacher in the system), you serve as the primary academic mentor for assigned students during their internships.

#### Q3: How are students assigned to me?
**A:** Students are assigned by the Principal through the **Mentor Assignment** module. You cannot self-assign students.

### Student Supervision

#### Q4: How many students will I supervise?
**A:** The number of students per Faculty Supervisor is decided by the Principal based on institutional needs.

#### Q5: How do I view my assigned students?
**A:** Your dashboard displays an **Assigned Students** table with all students under your supervision. You can also navigate to **Student Progress** for detailed tracking.

#### Q6: What are my key responsibilities?
**A:** Your key responsibilities include:
- Conducting monthly visits (physical, virtual, or telephonic)
- Monitoring internship progress and milestone completion
- Verifying student-submitted documents
- Handling and resolving student grievances
- Providing guidance and support throughout the internship

### Visits & Documentation

#### Q7: How often must I visit students?
**A:** You must conduct at least 1 visit per month for each assigned student. The total number of required visits equals the number of internship months.

#### Q8: What types of visits are allowed?
**A:** Three types of visits are permitted:
- Physical visits to the internship site
- Virtual visits (video calls)
- Telephonic visits (phone calls)

#### Q9: How do I log a visit?
**A:** Navigate to **Faculty Supervision > Log Visits** to record visit details including date, type, observations, and student feedback.

#### Q10: What should I include in visit logs?
**A:** Visit logs should include:
- Visit date and type
- Student performance observations
- Work environment assessment
- Any concerns or issues noted
- Follow-up actions required

#### Q11: Can I edit a visit log after submission?
**A:** Check with your Principal or system administrator about edit capabilities. Generally, visit logs should be accurate upon submission.

### Grievance Management

#### Q12: How do I handle student grievances?
**A:** Navigate to **Faculty Supervision > My Grievances** to view, respond to, and resolve grievances assigned to you by students.

#### Q13: What types of grievances might I receive?
**A:** Common grievances include:
- Workplace environment issues
- Task assignment concerns
- Communication problems with industry supervisors
- Safety concerns
- Compensation or stipend issues

#### Q14: How quickly should I respond to grievances?
**A:** Respond to grievances promptly, ideally within 24-48 hours. Escalate serious issues to the Principal if needed.

### Progress Tracking

#### Q15: How do I monitor student document submissions?
**A:** Your dashboard shows:
- **Joining/Offer Letter Status:** Submitted vs. pending
- **Monthly Reports:** Total submitted vs. expected
- **Pending Monthly Reports:** Count requiring follow-up

#### Q16: What do I do if a student hasn't submitted required documents?
**A:** Follow up directly with the student via phone, email, or the system's communication tools. Document your follow-up attempts.

#### Q17: How do I verify submitted documents?
**A:** Access student documents through the **Student Progress** module. Review documents for completeness and authenticity, then mark them as verified in the system.

### Student Registration

#### Q18: Can I register new students?
**A:** Yes, Faculty members have the ability to register students. Navigate to **Students > Register Student** for individual registration or **Bulk Register Student** for Excel uploads.

#### Q19: How do I send login credentials to students?
**A:** After registering students, navigate to **Students > Send Credentials** to email login details to one or multiple students.

## Principal FAQs

### Administrative Overview

#### Q1: How is my Principal account created?
**A:** Principal accounts are created by the **State Directorate**. Login credentials are sent to your official email address.

#### Q2: What is my role in the system?
**A:** As Principal, you serve as the institutional leader responsible for overseeing all aspects of the college internship program, including faculty management, student management, and institutional performance monitoring.

#### Q3: What are my key responsibilities?
**A:** Your key responsibilities include:
- Monitoring institutional performance across all students and faculty
- Creating staff accounts and managing faculty members
- Assigning faculty mentors to students
- Handling escalated student grievances
- Tracking institutional analytics and performance metrics
- Resetting staff passwords when needed

### Faculty Management

#### Q4: How do I create faculty accounts?
**A:** Navigate to **Administration > Create Staff** to create new faculty accounts. Enter required details and credentials will be sent to the faculty member's email.

#### Q5: Can I edit faculty information?
**A:** Yes, access **Administration > Staff List** to view all staff members. Click on a staff member to edit their information.

#### Q6: How do I assign students to faculty mentors?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Management > Mentor Assignment** to assign students to faculty supervisors. You can assign multiple students to one faculty member.

#### Q7: How many students should I assign to each faculty member?
**A:** The number depends on your institutional capacity and faculty availability. Consider workload balance and ensure quality supervision. A typical ratio might be 10-20 students per faculty member, but this varies by institution.

#### Q8: Can I reassign a student to a different mentor?
**A:** Yes, access the **Mentor Assignment** module to change faculty assignments. Ensure proper communication with both the previous and new mentor.

#### Q9: How do I reset a faculty member's password?
**A:** Navigate to **Administration > Staff List**, select the faculty member, and use the password reset function. The new credentials will be sent to their registered email.

### Student Management

#### Q10: How do I register students?
**A:** You have two options:
- **Individual Registration:** Navigate to **Students > Register Student**
- **Bulk Registration:** Navigate to **Students > Bulk Register Student** to upload an Excel file with multiple student records

#### Q11: What information is required for student registration?
**A:** Typical required fields include:
- Registration Number (unique identifier)
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Contact Number
- Program/Course
- Batch/Year

#### Q12: Can I view all students in my institution?
**A:** Yes, navigate to **Students > All Students** to view the complete student directory with filtering and search capabilities.

#### Q13: How do I send credentials to newly registered students?
**A:** After registration, navigate to **Students > Send Credentials** to email login details to students. You can send credentials individually or in bulk.

### Analytics & Monitoring

#### Q14: Where can I view institutional performance metrics?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Management > Analytics & Reports** (Dashboard) to access comprehensive institutional analytics and KPIs.

#### Q15: What key metrics should I monitor?
**A:** Key institutional metrics include:
- Total students with active internships
- Joining letter submission rates
- Monthly report submission rates
- Faculty visit completion rates
- Grievance resolution rates
- Overall internship completion rates

#### Q16: How do I track individual student progress?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Management > Student Progress** to view detailed progress tracking for individual students, including milestone completion and document submissions.

#### Q17: How do I monitor faculty performance?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Management > Faculty Progress** to track faculty supervision activities, visit logs, and student assignment status.

### Grievance Management

#### Q18: What types of grievances will I see?
**A:** As Principal, you handle escalated grievances that faculty mentors couldn't resolve, serious institutional issues, or complaints about faculty members.

#### Q19: How do I access student grievances?
**A:** Navigate to **Internship Management > Student Grievances** to view, respond to, and resolve escalated complaints.

#### Q20: What is my role in grievance resolution?
**A:** You serve as the final authority for grievance resolution at the institutional level. Your responsibilities include:
- Reviewing escalated grievances
- Investigating serious complaints
- Taking corrective actions
- Communicating resolutions to all parties
- Escalating to State Directorate if necessary

### Role & Permission Management

#### Q21: How do I manage user roles?
**A:** Navigate to **Administration > Assign Roles** to manage user roles and permissions within your institution.

#### Q22: What roles can I create or modify?
**A:** You can typically create and manage:
- Faculty/Teacher accounts
- Student accounts
- Administrative staff accounts (if supported)

#### Q23: Can I give a faculty member additional permissions?
**A:** Role permissions are typically predefined by the system. Contact State Directorate support if you need custom role configurations.

## Technical Support

### General Technical Issues

#### Q1: The page is not loading properly. What should I do?
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Refresh the page (Ctrl + F5 or Cmd + Shift + R)
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. If the issue persists, submit a technical query through the portal

#### Q2: I'm getting an error message. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Take a screenshot of the error message
2. Note what action you were performing when the error occurred
3. Navigate to **Support & Help > Submit Technical Query**
4. Provide detailed information including the screenshot
5. Include your role, timestamp, and steps to reproduce the issue

#### Q3: My uploaded file is not showing. What could be wrong?
**A:** Check these common issues:
- File size exceeds the maximum limit
- File format is not supported (use PDF, DOC, or DOCX)
- Internet connection was interrupted during upload
- Browser compatibility issues
Try uploading again. If the problem persists, submit a technical query.

#### Q4: How do I submit a technical query?
**A:** All user roles have access to **Support & Help > Submit Technical Query**. Fill in:
- Issue category
- Detailed description
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- Priority level

#### Q5: How can I track my technical query status?
**A:** Navigate to **Support & Help > My Queries** to view all your submitted queries and their current status (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Closed).

### Account Issues

#### Q6: I cannot log in. What should I do?
**A:** Verify:
1. You're using the correct username (Registration Number for students)
2. Password is entered correctly (check Caps Lock)
3. Your account is active
4. You're accessing the correct portal URL: https://placeintern.com/login
If issues persist, contact your supervisor for account verification.

#### Q7: My account seems to be locked. What happened?
**A:** Accounts may be locked after multiple failed login attempts for security. Contact your immediate supervisor:
- Students: Contact Faculty Mentor
- Faculty: Contact Principal
- Principal: Contact State Directorate

#### Q8: I didn't receive my login credentials email. What should I do?
**A:** Check:
1. Your spam/junk folder
2. Email address is correctly registered in the system
3. Contact the person who created your account (Principal or State Directorate)

### Data & Reporting Issues

#### Q9: My data is not displaying correctly. What should I do?
**A:** 
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try logging out and logging back in
4. If specific data is missing, verify it was submitted correctly
5. Submit a technical query with specific details about the missing or incorrect data

#### Q10: I cannot export or download reports. What's wrong?
**A:** Ensure:
- You have the necessary permissions for the report
- Pop-up blockers are disabled
- Browser allows file downloads
- You have sufficient storage space
If issues continue, try a different browser or submit a technical query.

### Mobile & Accessibility

#### Q11: Can I access the portal from my mobile phone?
**A:** Yes, the portal should be accessible from mobile browsers. However, some features may work better on desktop browsers. Use Chrome or Safari on mobile devices for best experience.

#### Q12: The portal is not responsive on my tablet. What should I do?
**A:** The portal is primarily designed for desktop use. If you encounter issues on tablets:
1. Try rotating the screen to landscape mode
2. Use desktop mode in your mobile browser
3. For critical tasks, access the portal from a desktop computer

### File Upload Issues

#### Q13: What file types can I upload?
**A:** Generally accepted formats include:
- PDF (.pdf)
- Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
- Images (.jpg, .jpeg, .png) for profile photos
Check specific upload sections for any format restrictions.

#### Q14: What is the maximum file size for uploads?
**A:** File size limits vary by document type. Typical limits are:
- Documents: 5-10 MB
- Images: 2-5 MB
If your file is too large, try compressing it or saving it at a lower quality.

#### Q15: My file upload keeps failing. What should I do?
**A:**
1. Check your internet connection stability
2. Verify file size is within limits
3. Ensure file format is supported
4. Try uploading from a different browser
5. Rename the file if it contains special characters
6. Submit a technical query if the issue persists

### Performance Issues

#### Q16: The portal is running slowly. How can I improve performance?
**A:**
1. Close unnecessary browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Check your internet speed
5. Try accessing during off-peak hours
6. Update your browser to the latest version

#### Q17: Who do I contact for urgent technical issues?
**A:** For urgent technical issues that impact critical deadlines:
1. Submit a high-priority technical query through the portal
2. Email dtepunjab.internship@gmail.com with "URGENT" in the subject line
3. Contact your immediate supervisor (Faculty Mentor or Principal)
